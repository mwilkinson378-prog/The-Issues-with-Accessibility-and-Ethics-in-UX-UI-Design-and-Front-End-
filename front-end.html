#SYSTEM_LOG_002

Front-End
Accessibility & Ethics

Front end development is not neutral execution. It is a functional layer that actively mediates human behavior and determines how users interact with online environments. Digital code has the power to expand autonomy through a transparent framework and inclusivity, while making sure websites meet standards. Front end implementation can destroy autonomy through technical execution "accessibility barriers" that effectively lock out users. This can deploy dark patterns and practices that exploit psychological.

MANIFESTO_DATA_LOG_001

The Architecture
of Impact

In the modern digital production of coding, conflict exists where the drive for high engagement metrics and stakeholder profit frequently takes priority over user accessibility and ethics. Research indicated that the online landscape is now crowded with code engineered to have a lack of autonomy.

This can result in negative user safety and behavior. The trends signal a breakdown of ethics. This leads to tactics of "attention theft" or "coercive friction" and reveals a symptom of indifferent user intentions, and choosing psychological vulnerabilities for users.

These are not by mistake, as noted in studies from Virginia Tech, these interfaces are deliberately "crafted to manipulate" users into unintended behaviors.While developers acknowledge that external pressures often mandate the deployment of these deceptive elements, the ethical accountability for such "hostile designs" ultimately rests with the creators of the technology.
